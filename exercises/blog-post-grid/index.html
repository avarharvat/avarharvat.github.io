Appenzell, Switzerland: Top things to do & best hikes(2023)


Appenzell is a hidden gem in Switzerland. Sinuous rocky ridges and blue-green lakes provide a backdrop for some excellent hiking. Here’s what to see and do in Appenzell.



While tourists flock to Zermatt and the Berner Oberland, Appenzell remains surprisingly quiet.The mountains may not be as high or the valleys as deep, but the sharp ridges and serrated rock edges make this an excellent hiking destination.


It’s also accessible. Cable cars provide easy access to excellent hikes, breathtaking views, and scenic huts carved into the rock. If you’re a fan of the dramatic spiky peaks and alpine meadows like those of the Italian Dolomites, then Appenzell might be a great destination for you.
